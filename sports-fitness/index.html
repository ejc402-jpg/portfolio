A Statistical Approach to the BWF World Championships - September 2nd, 2025

With so many factors on and off the court that affect the outcome of a single match, can we utilize data and machine learning techniques to develop a consistent method to predicting success at the BWF World Championships?

This project takes in match data from 2018 to 2021 and attempts to form connections between the attributes of winners and losers during a Word Tour tournament schedule.
